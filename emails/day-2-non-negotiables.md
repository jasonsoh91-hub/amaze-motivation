**TODAY'S EXERCISE: Your Non-Negotiables**

A "non-negotiable" is something you're no longer willing to compromise on. Period.

Write down 5-7 answers to this question:

**"What do I refuse to settle for in my life?"**

Some examples to get you thinking:

- "I refuse to settle for relationships where I have to shrink myself to fit in."
- "I refuse to settle for work that drains my soul 5 days a week."
- "I refuse to settle for feeling disconnected from my own body."
- "I refuse to settle for 'fine' when I know I want 'fulfilled.'"

Your turn. Be specific. Be ruthless.

---

**THE "HELL YES" FILTER**

Here's how you use this going forward:

- If it's not a "HELL YES"—it's a "no."

- If you feel "maybe" about something—that's a polite way of saying "no."

- You don't owe anyone an explanation. "No" is a full sentence.
