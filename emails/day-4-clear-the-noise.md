**THE GUILT TRAP**

You know why we say yes to everything we don't want to do?

Guilt.

We're afraid of:
- Disappointing people
- Being called "selfish"
- Missing out
- Not being "enough" if we don't do it all

But here's what I've learned:

**The people who matter will respect your boundaries. The people who don't? They're not your people to begin with.**

---

**TODAY'S EXERCISE: Clear The Noise**

Look at your calendar and your commitments over the next month.

Ask yourself these questions about each one:

1. "Did I say yes because I wanted to, or because I felt obligated?"

2. "If I said no to this, what would actually happen?" (Be real with yourself)

3. "Does this align with any of my non-negotiables from Day 2?"

4. "Am I doing this out of love, or out of guilt?"

For every "wrong" answer—consider canceling, delegating, or respectfully declining.
