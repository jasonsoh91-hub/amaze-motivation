**TODAY: Your Daily Clarity Practice**

I want to give you a simple 5-minute morning practice that will keep you connected to what matters, every single day.

You can do this with coffee, before you check your phone, in a journal—whatever works.

**THE 5 DAILY QUESTIONS:**

1. **"What's my ONE main focus today?"**
   (Not everything. Just the thing that moves the needle most.)

2. **"How do I want to feel today?"**
   (Stressed? Rushed? Or grounded, intentional, energized? Set the tone.)

3. **"What's one thing I can do for myself today?"**
   (Not for your job, not for your family—for YOU. You matter.)

4. **"What's one energy drain I can avoid or minimize today?"**
   (That meeting? That person? That distraction. Protect your energy.)

5. **"What's one thing I'm grateful for right now?"**
   (Gratitude shifts everything. Start here.)

That's it. Five questions. Five minutes.

Do this every morning for 30 days and watch your life change.
